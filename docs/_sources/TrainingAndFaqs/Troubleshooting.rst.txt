Troubleshooting
=================


Login errors:

.. image:: /images/login_error.png
   :width: 600
   :alt: Image of Open OnDemand login error.

This error occurs when you choose to login with a provider that doesn't provide some necessary information back to Open OnDemand. If you see this error you may need to clear cookies and login with a new provider. We recommend logging in with your UW-Madison account if possible. If you continue to experience this error please contact cluster support: jfscheuren@wisc.edu.
