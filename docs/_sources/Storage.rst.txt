Storage on the HPC Cluster
============================

User files for the HPC cluster are stored on a Ceph provides POSIX filesystem storage.

To transfer files to the cluster:
1. Login to https://www.globus.org/
2. Search for the UW-Madison Cryo-Electron Microscopy Center HPC Access collection.
3. Click on the folder with your username.
4. Drag and drop files or use the Upload link on the right sidebar.

Your files should now be accessible in your home directory on the cluster.
